About Me

My name is Ryan Ellingson. I recently began transitioning to a career in web development after obtaining a degree in Chemical Engineering and working for 4 years as a process engineer in the chemical industry. Whether it be understanding and correcting a problem that resulted in a process safety incident, designing and building a new blending operation in a latex plant, or programming a cool web app from scratch, I love simultaneously using my analytical skills and creativity to solve challenging problems. Like a sculptor bringing life to a block of marble, I find there are few things in life as satisfying as taking a concept from your mind and making it a reality.

Outside of work I enjoy playing the bass, hiking, and homebrewing. In the last few years I started visiting fun parts of the country with my dad, including the Grand Canyon, Sedona, AZ, and Route 66. I also like video games, and have particularly been a fan of role-playing games like Final Fantasy, Kingdom Hearts, and Dark Souls over the years.
